Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What kind of cases do you take?


A: I only take on matters involving assisted or third-party reproduction, which includes everything from surrogacy contracts, sperm or egg donation contracts, and obtaining pre-birth orders of parentage.

Q: Do you only represent clients in Louisville?


No! I am licensed in Kentucky and I have proudly represented clients from all over the Commonwealth.

Q: Do you offer free consultations?


A: Absolutely! Consultations are free and once we agree on representation all of my services come at a flat rate depending on the particular needs we discuss during the consultation!

Q: Do you offer evening or weekend appointments?


A: I do my best to accomodate my clients' schedule, so I only offer evening or weekend appointments. All appointments are also conducted via phone or video conferencing platforms, like Google Meet.
